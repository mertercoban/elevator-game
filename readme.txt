The elevator has capacity of 4 people,
there are 8 people on the building, the building has 4 floors (0-1-2-3).

If a person wants to use the elevator, they are rendered on the left side of the elevator.
If there are room for them, they move towards the elevator then enter.

If a person reached their target, they are rendered on the right side of the elevator
with a message about them.
